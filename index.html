Our Services

Single Family Homes

An inspection of a single family home will include the outside, all living levels in side, the attic if accessible, and the basement. In addition, detached structures on the property also can be inspected.

Multi-Family Homes

Part of owning a multi-family property is proper planning. Multi-family properties like any property need care and maintenance. O’s inspection will provide you the information you need to plan for the future of your investment.

Condo / Townhouses

O’s will inspect the interior of the condo. In addition, when the common areas, roof, and utility rooms are available for review and investigation they are also inspected.

Commercial Property

Otis’ experience with more than 20 years in the construction industry matched with his inspection expertise provide for the competency you need in a commercial property inspector.
